School catchment areas

This map shows catchment areas for a selection of secondary schools in Waltham Forest, using cut off distances as applied for intake in September 2010.

Notes

  • The colours of the map pins and the associated circles are randomly assigned. If any particular pin is too dark, then the page can be re-loaded in order to assign new colours
  • Not all schools are currently included on the map
  • Some schools (those which are not over-subscribed) do not have a distance cut-off
  • The distance data is taken from Moving on - a guide to applying for a secondary school place for September 2012 published by the London Borough of Waltham Forest
